# privacy policy

*effective date: 17 february, 2026*

your privacy matters. this policy explains what information i collect, why i collect it, and what rights you have with respect to that information.

## 1. information i collect

i only collect information you choose to share with me.

### account information

if you create an account, i collect:

- a username

- a password of your choosing

- an email address (optional)

- a phone number (optional)

your email address is stored and used solely for password recovery. i do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional messages.

passwords are stored securely using appropriate hashing and security practices.

phone numbers strictly serve as a tie from your phone to your mrndm account and memo database. i do not send marketing texts or promotional messages.

### content you choose to submit

any information you voluntarily save to mrndm's database is submitted at your own discretion. this information is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and i do not have the ability to view the original content of your messages. i do not analyze, sell, or repurpose this content, but it is (securely) stored on mrndm's server.

memos you save to mrndm are not shared publicly in any way, shape, or form.

## 2. information i do not collect

- i do not collect personal information from visitors who do not create an account

- i do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics

- i do not collect location data, payment information, or social media data

- i do not sell, rent, or trade personal information

## 3. how your information is used

your information is used only to:

- create and maintain your account

- allow you to log in and recover your password (should you choose to share your email)

- allow you to save your memos via your phone (should you choose to opt-in to `mrndm linkphone`)

i do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

## 4. legal bases for processing (g.d.p.r.)

if you are located in the european economic area, the legal bases for processing your personal data are:

- your consent, when you choose to create an account

- the performance of a contract, namely providing access to your account and memos

you may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account.

## 5. your rights under g.d.p.r.

if you are located in the e.e.a., you have the right to:

- access the personal data i have about you

- request correction of inaccurate data

- request deletion of your personal data

- request restriction or object to processing

- request a copy of your data in a portable format

to exercise these rights, contact me using the information below.

## 6. your rights under c.c.p.a. (california residents)

if you are a california resident, you have the right to:

- know what personal information is collected about you

- request deletion of your personal information

- confirm that your personal information is not sold

i do not sell personal information. requests may be made using the contact information below.

## 7. data retention

account information is retained only for as long as your account remains active. if you delete your account, your account information will be removed within a reasonable timeframe.

## 8. data security

reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect your data. however, no system is perfectly secure, and i cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. third parties

i do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law.

## 10. changes to this policy

this privacy policy may be updated from time to time. any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

## 11. contact

if you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, you may contact me at `nic (at) plots.club`.
